# UnidBox Lifecycle Management - Improvement Plan

## Current State Analysis

### Dealer-Side (My Products Page)
**Current Features:**
- Basic lifecycle tracking with 4 stages
- Product cards with progress bars
- Filter by status
- Product Care modal
- One-click reorder

**Gaps Identified:**
1. No visual timeline showing product journey
2. No maintenance history tracking
3. No cost analysis (TCO - Total Cost of Ownership)
4. No warranty tracking
5. No bulk actions for multiple products
6. Limited predictive insights

### Admin-Side
**Current Features:**
- None (no admin dashboard exists)

**Gaps Identified:**
1. No visibility into dealer lifecycle patterns
2. No aggregate analytics on product health
3. No proactive engagement triggers
4. No revenue forecasting from lifecycle events
5. No dealer segmentation by lifecycle behavior

---

## Proposed Improvements

### A. Enhanced Dealer Lifecycle Features

#### 1. Product Timeline View
- Visual timeline showing: Purchase → Installation → Maintenance Events → EOL
- Clickable events with details
- Projected future events

#### 2. Maintenance History Log
- Track all maintenance activities
- Add notes and photos
- Service provider tracking

#### 3. Warranty Tracker
- Warranty expiration dates
- Claim history
- Extended warranty options

#### 4. Cost Analysis Dashboard
- Total Cost of Ownership per product
- Maintenance costs over time
- Projected replacement costs
- ROI calculations

#### 5. Bulk Actions
- Select multiple products
- Bulk reorder
- Bulk schedule maintenance
- Export lifecycle report

#### 6. Smart Alerts Configuration
- Customize notification preferences
- Set custom maintenance intervals
- Snooze reminders

### B. Admin Analytics Dashboard

#### 1. Lifecycle Overview
- Total products tracked across all dealers
- Distribution by lifecycle stage
- Trend over time (improving/declining)

#### 2. Dealer Engagement Metrics
- Active dealers vs dormant
- Average products per dealer
- Lifecycle feature adoption rate

#### 3. Revenue Opportunity Dashboard
- Predicted reorder revenue (next 30/60/90 days)
- Maintenance service revenue potential
- At-risk revenue (dealers with EOL products not reordering)

#### 4. Product Health Heatmap
- Which products have highest failure rates
- Average lifecycle by product category
- Maintenance frequency analysis

#### 5. Proactive Engagement Queue
- Dealers needing outreach
- Prioritized by revenue potential
- Suggested talking points

#### 6. Predictive Analytics
- Churn risk by dealer
- Reorder probability scores
- Seasonal demand forecasting
